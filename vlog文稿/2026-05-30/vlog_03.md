## 课本不教但雅思口语必会的表达

### 态度与观点

| 表达 | 中文释义 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| I'm on the fence | 我还在犹豫/骑墙 | I'm on the fence about which university to choose. |
| Fair enough | 说得有道理/行吧 | "I just need more time." "Fair enough, take your time." |
| I can't be bothered | 懒得/不想费事 | I can't be bothered to cook tonight — let's order in. |
| It's not my thing | 我不太喜欢/不适合我 | Camping is just not my thing, I prefer hotels. |
| I'm good | 不用了/我ok | "Want more coffee?" "I'm good, thanks." |
| Suit yourself | 随你便/你开心就好 | "I'll wear the red one." "Suit yourself." |

### 日常生活

| 表达 | 中文释义 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| Grab a bite | 随便吃点东西 | Wanna grab a bite before the movie? |
| Crash at someone's place | 在某人那里过夜/借宿 | My flight's cancelled, can I crash at your place? |
| Swing by | 顺路去一下/路过 | I'll swing by the store on my way home. |
| Run errands | 跑腿/办事 | I spent the whole morning running errands. |
| Zone out | 走神/发呆 | I totally zoned out during the meeting. |
| Wing it | 即兴发挥/临场应付 | I didn't prepare my speech, I'll just wing it. |

## 雅思口语提示

这20+个表达在雅思口语Part 1和Part 2中非常实用。考官特别喜欢听到"自然英语"而非"课本英语"。
试着在回答中自然穿插1-2个这样的短语，能让你的口语分数明显提升。

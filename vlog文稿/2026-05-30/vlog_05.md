# 旅行Vlog英语 — 跟博主学最地道的出行表达
**来源**: 多公众号综合（跟着Miss Honey、WordWalk漫游记、旅游英语急救包）

## 一、旅行Vlog高频动词短语

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Take in the view | 欣赏风景 | Let's just stop here and take in the view for a moment. |
| Head to / Head out | 前往/出发 | We're heading to the beach — you coming? |
| Check it out | 快看/瞧瞧这个 | Check it out — you can see the whole city from here! |
| Scope out | 提前踩点/侦查 | We scoped out the best coffee shops before the trip. |
| Stumble upon | 偶然发现 | We stumbled upon this amazing little restaurant. |
| Hit the road | 出发上路 | Alright everyone, let's hit the road! |
| Swing by | 顺路去一下 | Let's swing by that viewpoint before heading back. |
| Bundle up | 裹得严严实实 | It's freezing out there — bundle up! |
| Wind down | 放松下来/逐步结束 | After a full day of sightseeing, we're winding down at a rooftop bar. |

## 二、旅行场景地道短句

| 表达 | 中文释义 | 场景 |
|------|----------|------|
| I swear. | 我发誓/说真的！ | 赞叹美景时的强调语气 |
| Trust me, it's harder than it looks. | 相信我，比看起来难多了 | 体验类活动后 |
| This is my happy place. | 这就是我最开心的时刻/地方 | 表达极度满足 |
| I can barely contain myself. | 我快控制不住自己了（兴奋） | 极度期待某事 |
| The view is breathtaking. | 美景令人窒息 | 赞叹景色 |
| Time flies so fast. | 时间过得好快 | 旅行即将结束时 |
| Let's cherish the moment. | 我们珍惜这一刻 | 与同伴分享时光 |
| I haven't ___ in ages. | 我好久没___了 | 重游故地/重温某事 |

## 三、2026年旅行英语新词

| 新词 | 中文释义 | 说明 |
|------|----------|------|
| Cool-cation | 清凉度假/避暑旅行 | cool + vacation，避开酷暑的旅行 |
| Set-jetting | 影视同款旅行 | 去电影/电视剧拍摄地打卡 |
| Glow-cation | 焕亮度假 | 以美容/养生/spa为主题的假期 |
| Micro-retirement | 微退休旅行 | 趁年轻暂时"退休"几个月去旅行 |
| Off-the-beaten-path | 小众路线/人迹罕至 | 避开旅游团的小众景点 |
| Once-in-a-lifetime | 一生一次的体验 | 极光、看鲸鱼、跳伞等 |

## 四、旅行Vlog常用句式框架

| 句式模板 | 填入你自己的内容 |
|----------|------------------|
| It's been ___ since I ___ | It's been years since I last went camping. |
| I can't get over how ___ | I can't get over how blue the water is! |
| If you ever get the chance to ___, do it. | If you ever get the chance to visit Iceland, do it. |
| Nothing beats ___ | Nothing beats a sunset on the beach. |
| This totally exceeded my expectations. | (通用赞叹句) |
| ___ was an absolute highlight. | The food tour was an absolute highlight. |

## 五、旅行金句

> The journey matters as much as the destination.
> 重要的不只是终点，路途本身也值得好好体验。

> Collect moments, not things.
> 收集回忆，而非物件。

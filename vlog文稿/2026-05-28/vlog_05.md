## 学习话题·40个地道表达（精选20个）

|------|----------|------|
| Hit the books | 用功读书 | Finals are coming, time to hit the books. |
| Burn the midnight oil | 熬夜学习 | I've been burning the midnight oil for this exam. |
| Ace a test | 考得很好 | I aced my chemistry test! |
| Cram for | 临时抱佛脚 | I spent all night cramming for the history final. |
| Brush up on | 温习/复习 | I need to brush up on my French before the trip. |
| Fall behind | 落后 | I missed two weeks of class and fell behind. |
| Catch on | 理解/跟上 | It took me a while to catch on to the new software. |
| Drop out | 辍学/退出 | He dropped out of college to start his business. |
| Sign up for | 报名参加 | I signed up for a photography workshop. |

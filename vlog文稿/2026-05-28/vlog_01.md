Good morning beautiful people. Rise and shine. Today is the first day back to school since my readjustment. No more procrastination, no more excuses. I'm going to get up, make my bed, and get ready for the day.

**Part II — Getting Ready**
I'm not really a morning person, to be honest. But I've found that if I just force myself to get up and not hit snooze, the rest of the day flows so much better. So the first thing I do is wash my face, brush my teeth, and do my skincare routine.

**Part III — Breakfast**
I don't usually have a big breakfast. Today I'm just going to grab something quick — maybe a smoothie and a granola bar. Nothing fancy. I'll eat on the go because I'm running a bit late.

**Part IV — Commute**
I take the bus to campus every day. It's about a 20-minute ride, which is the perfect amount of time to listen to a podcast or review my notes. Today I'm listening to a true crime podcast — it's been my guilty pleasure lately.

**Part V — Reflection**
Looking back, I think the key to a productive day isn't about waking up at 5 AM. It's about being intentional with your time, no matter when you start. So don't beat yourself up if you're not a morning person. Just make the most of the hours you have.

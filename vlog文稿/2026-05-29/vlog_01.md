## 完整场景词汇

### 场景一：机场 (Airport)

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Check-in counter | 值机柜台 | Excuse me, where can I check in for Flight CA123 to London? |
| Boarding pass | 登机牌 | Please have your boarding pass and passport ready. |
| Carry-on baggage | 手提行李 | Is this bag allowed as carry-on, or do I need to check it? |
| Checked baggage | 托运行李 | I have one suitcase to check in. |
| Baggage claim | 行李提取处 | Let's head to baggage claim — carousel 5. |
| Security check | 安检 | The security check line is really long today. |
| Departure gate | 登机口 | Our flight departs from Gate B23. |
| Stopover / layover | 中途停留/转机 | I have a 3-hour layover in Dubai. |
| Window seat / aisle seat | 靠窗/过道座位 | May I have a window seat, please? |
| Flight delayed | 航班延误 | My flight has been delayed. Do you know the new departure time? |
| Duty-free shop | 免税店 | Let's check out the duty-free shop before boarding. |
| Lost and found | 失物招领处 | I left my jacket on the plane — where's the lost and found? |

### 场景二：酒店 (Hotel)

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Make a reservation | 预订 | I made a reservation under the name Zhang. |
| Check in / Check out | 入住/退房 | I'd like to check in, please. |
| Room rate | 房价 | Is breakfast included in the room rate? |
| Wi-Fi password | Wi-Fi密码 | What's the Wi-Fi password? |
| Room service | 客房服务 | I'd like to order something from room service. |
| Wake-up call | 叫醒服务 | I'd like a wake-up call at 7 a.m. |
| Late check-out | 延迟退房 | Can I request a late check-out until 2 PM? |
| Leave luggage | 寄存行李 | Can I leave my luggage here after check-out? |
| Travel light | 轻装出行 | I always try to travel light — just a backpack. |
| Does my luggage go straight to my final destination? | 行李直挂 | This is a connecting flight — does my luggage go straight to the final destination? |

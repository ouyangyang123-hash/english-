## 按情绪分类的地道表达

### 开心/积极 (Positive Feelings)

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Over the moon | 高兴得不得了 | I'm over the moon about getting the job offer! |
| Made my day | 让我一整天都开心 | Your text really made my day. |
| Have a blast | 玩得很开心 | We had a blast at the concert last night. |
| Thrilled | 非常兴奋 | I'm absolutely thrilled about the trip! |
| A sense of achievement | 成就感 | Finishing that marathon gave me a huge sense of achievement. |

### 压力/紧张 (Stress & Anxiety)

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| On edge | 紧张不安 | I've been on edge ever since the interview was scheduled. |
| Under a lot of pressure | 压力很大 | I'm under a lot of pressure with the deadline approaching. |
| Overwhelmed | 应接不暇/不堪重负 | I feel so overwhelmed with all these tasks. |
| Burned out | 精疲力竭 | I was totally burned out after finals week. |
| Cool down | 冷静下来 | I need a moment to cool down. |

### 失望/沮丧 (Disappointment & Sadness)

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| In a funk | 情绪低落 | I've been in a funk all week — need to snap out of it. |
| Let down | 失望 | I was a bit let down by the movie ending. |
| Down in the dumps | 心情跌入谷底 | He's been down in the dumps since he lost his job. |
| Emotionally draining | 精神疲惫的 | Taking care of a newborn is emotionally draining. |
| Feel blue | 感到忧郁 | I always feel a bit blue on rainy days. |

### 生气/惊讶 (Anger & Surprise)

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Fuming mad | 气炸了 | He was fuming mad when he found out he'd been lied to. |
| Taken aback | 大吃一惊 | I was completely taken aback by how generous they were. |
| Mixed feelings | 百感交集 | I have mixed feelings about moving to a new city. |
| Shook | 震惊的 | I was shook when I heard the news. |
| That really bothered me | 那真的让我很困扰 | The way he spoke to her really bothered me. |

## 地道句型
- "When I feel stressed, I try to calm myself by taking a break or talking to someone I trust."
- "It was a tough day emotionally, but tomorrow will be better."
- "I have mixed feelings about it — excited but also a little nervous."

## 1. 出行旅游Vlog英语 — 机场+酒店全场景
🔗 来源: 微信公众号「旅行英语急救包」/ ELSA Speak旅游英文
📂 话题：Travel

### 词汇表
| 词汇/短语 | 中文释义 | 原文例句 |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Check-in counter | 值机柜台 | Excuse me, where can I check in for Flight CA123 to London? |
| Boarding pass | 登机牌 | Please have your boarding pass and passport ready. |
| Carry-on baggage | 手提行李 | Is this bag allowed as carry-on, or do I need to check it? |
| Baggage claim | 行李提取处 | Let's head to baggage claim — carousel 5. |
| Stopover / layover | 中途停留/转机 | I have a 3-hour layover in Dubai. |
| Make a reservation | 预订 | I made a reservation under the name Zhang. |
| Room rate | 房价 | Is breakfast included in the room rate? |
| Late check-out | 延迟退房 | Can I request a late check-out until 2 PM? |
| Travel light | 轻装出行 | I always try to travel light — just a backpack. |

### 地道句型
- "My flight has been delayed. Do you know the new departure time?" → 航班延误了，您知道新的起飞时间吗？
- "I'd like a room with a nice view, please." → 我想要一间风景好的房间。
- "Does my luggage go straight to my final destination?" → 我的行李会直挂到最终目的地吗？

## 4. 情绪感受Vlog英语 — 告别"happy/sad"的高阶情感表达
🔗 来源: B站「雅思口语8.5 10个表达心情感受的地道俚语」/ 微信情绪表达合集
📂 话题：Emotions / Feelings

### 词汇表
| 词汇/短语 | 中文释义 | 原文例句 |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Over the moon | 高兴得不得了 | I'm over the moon about getting the job offer! |
| Made my day | 让我一整天都开心 | Your text really made my day. |
| In a funk | 情绪低落 | I've been in a funk all week — need to snap out of it. |
| On edge | 紧张不安 | I've been on edge ever since the interview was scheduled. |
| Let down | 失望 | I was a bit let down by the movie ending. |
| Fuming mad | 气炸了 | He was fuming mad when he found out he'd been lied to. |

## 5. BBC地道英语 · 2026年5月最新表达特辑
🔗 来源: BBC Learning English「地道英语」栏目 (2026年4-5月)
📂 话题：Daily Life / Work / Social

### 词汇表
| 词汇/短语 | 中文释义 | 原文例句 |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| In over your head | 力不从心/应付不来 | I'm so stressed — I'm in over my head with this project! |
| Think on your feet | 随机应变/临场发挥 | I lost my presentation just before the meeting, so I had to think on my feet. |
| Can't see the wood for the trees | 一叶障目/见树不见林 | You're overthinking it — you can't see the wood for the trees. |
| Flow state | 心流/忘我状态 | When I'm painting, I get into a total flow state and lose track of time. |
| Kick the can down the road | 逃避问题/拖延 | We need to stop kicking the can down the road and make a decision. |
| Cut corners | 偷工减料/走捷径 | If you cut corners on safety, someone will get hurt. |
| Lock in | 全神贯注/锁定目标 | It's time to lock in and finish this assignment. |
| Yap | 叽里呱啦说个不停 | She just yaps on about her cats for hours. |

### 地道句型
- "Don't overthink it. Sometimes you just need to go with the flow." → 别想太多了，有时候顺其自然就好。
- "Fair enough, I see your point." → 有道理，我理解你的观点了。

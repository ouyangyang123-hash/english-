## 完整健身词汇表

### 健身常用动作/表达

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Hit the gym | 去健身房 | I'm about to hit the gym — leg day today! |
| Work out | 健身/锻炼 | I try to work out at least four times a week. |
| Work up a sweat | 出汗/努力锻炼 | We really worked up a sweat in that HIIT class. |
| Stay in shape | 保持身材 | Jogging three times a week helps me stay in shape. |
| Out of shape | 身材走样 | I've been a bit out of shape lately. |
| Burn calories | 燃烧卡路里 | Swimming is a great way to burn calories. |
| Cardio | 有氧运动 | I usually start with 20 minutes of cardio. |
| Reps (repetitions) | 组数中的次数 | Do 3 sets of 12 reps for each exercise. |
| Sore muscles | 肌肉酸痛 | My legs are so sore from yesterday's squats. |
| Push through | 咬牙坚持 | Your legs are burning, but you've got to push through. |
| Boost my confidence | 增强自信 | Working out really boosted my confidence. |
| Without a doubt | 毫无疑问 | It improved my mental health, without a doubt. |
| Cool down | 冷却/放松 | Don't forget to cool down and stretch after your workout. |
| Wind down | 放松减压 | I like to wind down with some yoga after a workout. |
| Pull a muscle | 拉伤肌肉 | I think I pulled a muscle during deadlifts. |
| Hit legs / Hit upper body | 练腿/练上半身 | I'm hitting upper body today — chest and shoulders. |
| Glutes and hamstrings | 臀肌和腿筋 | Today's workout focuses on glutes and hamstrings. |

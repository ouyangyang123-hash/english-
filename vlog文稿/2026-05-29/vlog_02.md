## 场景一：餐厅点餐 (Ordering at a Restaurant)

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| I'd like to start with… | 我想先来一道…… | I'd like to start with the cheese platter as an appetizer. |
| Can I get…? | 我能来一份……吗？ | Can I get the grilled salmon? |
| I'll go with… | 我要点…… | I'll go with the daily special. |
| What's the specialty? | 你们的特色菜是什么？ | What's the specialty of the house? |
| What do you recommend? | 你推荐什么？ | What do you recommend for dessert? |
| Can I have a refill? | 可以续杯吗？ | Can I have a refill on my coffee? |
| Split the bill | AA制 | Let's split the bill equally. |
| I'll pick up the check | 我来买单 | Don't worry, I'll pick up the check. |
| It's my treat | 我请客 | Order whatever you like — it's my treat. |
| Dine in / Take-out | 堂食/外带 | Are you dining in or taking out? |

## 场景二：描述味道与评价 (Taste & Review)

| 表达 | 中文释义 | Vlog例句 |
|------|----------|----------|
| It's to die for! | 好吃到极致！ | This chocolate cake is to die for! |
| Finger-licking good | 好吃到吮指回味 | The fried chicken is finger-licking good. |
| Mouth-watering | 让人垂涎三尺 | The smell alone is mouth-watering. |
| It melts in your mouth | 入口即化 | This wagyu beef literally melts in your mouth. |
| Crispy outside, juicy inside | 外酥里嫩 | The fried tofu is crispy outside, juicy inside. |
| Nothing to write home about | 一般般/没啥特别 | The food was okay, but nothing to write home about. |
| A hidden gem | 宝藏餐厅 | This tiny ramen shop is a real hidden gem in the city. |
| Worth every penny | 物超所值 | A bit pricey, but worth every penny. |
| Didn't live up to the hype | 没有达到预期 | I had high hopes, but it didn't quite live up to the hype. |
| A solid 9 out of 10 | 可以打9分 | I'd give it a solid 9 out of 10. |
| I'm docking one star for… | 扣一颗星是因为…… | I'd dock one star for the long wait time. |
| Reasonably priced | 价格公道 | The lunch menu is surprisingly reasonably priced. |

---

## 地道句型
- "This place is a hidden gem — I can't believe I've been sleeping on it!"
- "Honest review: the salmon was to die for. I'd give it a solid 9 out of 10."
- "What's the specialty of the house? I want to try something unique."
